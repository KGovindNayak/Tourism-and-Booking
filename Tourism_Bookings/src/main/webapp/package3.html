irupathi


Tirupathi : Tirumala, located in the Eastern Ghats of Andhra Pradesh, India, holds immense religious significance as the abode of Lord Venkateswara, a form of Lord Vishnu. The ancient temple of Sri Venkateswara atop Tirumala hills is a major pilgrimage site and one of the richest temples globally
